My Type — Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Privacy policy

1. Introduction

My Type ("we", "our app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, shared, and protected when you use the My Type app.

2. Data we collect

To provide the service and improve your experience, we collect:

  • Account and profile information: Your name, age, gender, preferences, profile photos, and other bio details you provide.
  • In-app interaction data: Your likes, matches, messages, and in-app activity.
  • Location information: When you allow access in device settings, we may use foreground device location while the app is open to suggest your city and country for your profile and discovery. If you grant precise location, we may store approximate coordinates on your profile to improve matching; you can also enter your city manually. We do not use background location for continuous tracking.
  • Push notification data: A device push token and related technical data so we can deliver notifications (for example new messages or matches), where you have opted in and the OS allows it.
  • Sign-in and identity providers: If you use Apple, Google, or email-based sign-in, the relevant provider processes certain identity data under their own policies in addition to the data we store in our systems.
  • Technical and device information: IP address, device model, OS version, and app performance data.

3. How we use your data

We process data only for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account and provide access to the app.
  • To suggest suitable matches (including compatibility scoring and, where available, optional AI-generated match insights). For those insights, limited profile and match-related information may be sent to our AI subprocessors; results are shown in the app.
  • To enable secure communication and messaging between users.
  • To protect the app, prevent fake profiles, and enforce community rules.
  • To measure performance and fix errors.

4. Sharing and subprocessors

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared only in these cases:

  • Service providers (subprocessors): We use trusted infrastructure and product partners, including for example: Supabase (authentication, database, file storage, realtime features, and server-side functions), Expo (push notification delivery to your device), and AI inference providers (for example OpenRouter or similar) used only to generate optional in-app match insights. They process data only as needed to provide those services and under appropriate agreements.
  • Legal requirements: With authorities when required by a valid court order or legal request to meet our legal obligations.

Where personal data is transferred to countries outside your own, we rely on lawful transfer mechanisms required by applicable law (such as standard contractual clauses where applicable).

5. Account and data deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Account deletion: Permanently delete your account via "Settings > Delete account" in the app or by written request to support@mytypeapp.com.
  • Deletion of data: When you delete your account, your profile, photos, messages, and match data are permanently removed from our systems. Only essential security logs required by law may be retained in encrypted form for the period required by applicable laws.

6. Children's privacy

My Type is intended for users aged 18 and over. Our services are not directed at individuals under 18. If we learn we have collected data from someone under 18, we will delete that account and related data promptly.

7. Your rights

Depending on applicable data protection laws (e.g. Turkish KVKK, GDPR), you may have the right to access, rectify, delete, restrict processing, or request data portability.

8. Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. You acknowledge that no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, requests regarding your personal data, or account deletion, contact us at:

Email: support@mytypeapp.com
